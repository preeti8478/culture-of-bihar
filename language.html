Languages of Bihar

Maithili

Maithili is one of the most ancient languages of India, spoken widely in the Mithila region of Bihar. It is also included in the Eighth Schedule of the Indian Constitution.

Bhojpuri

Bhojpuri is spoken mainly in western Bihar and eastern Uttar Pradesh. It has a rich tradition of folk songs and is used widely in Bihar cinema and culture.

Magahi

Magahi is spoken in the Magadh region of Bihar. It has roots in the ancient language Magadhi Prakrit and is still used in many rural areas.

Angika

Angika is spoken in Bhagalpur and Munger areas of Bihar. It has its own cultural identity and is closely related to other Eastern Indo-Aryan languages.
