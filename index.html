Thekua

Thekua is famous in Bihar because it is a sacred offering prepared for Chhath Puja and dedicated to the Sun God. It is made using wheat flour, jaggery, coconut, and dry fruits, and is shaped by pressing the dough into wooden moulds to create beautiful designs.

Khaja

Khaja is a traditional sweet popular in the Gaya and Rajgir regions of Bihar It is offered as prasad during festivals, weddings, and in temples, and is loved for its crispy, layered texture.It is made from refined flour, ghee, and dipped in sugar syrup.

Dal Pitha

Dal Pitha is a traditional and healthy Bihari steamed snack made with rice flour dough filled with spiced chana dal, garlic, and ginger, usually prepared on special vegetarian occasions.
